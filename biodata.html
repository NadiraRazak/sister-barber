About The Sister's Barber 妹の床屋


      


Our Mission

Our mission is to ensure that at The Sister's Barber, any guy or girl who wakes up and thinks they wants a great haircut can get one. Oh, that day! Unlike every other store, we are dedicated to delivering excellent quality, professional customer service and an awesome experience.

We are committed to offering accessible, unparalleled hair styling and grooming services at The Sister's Barber that are customized to each customer and their lifestyle in a distinctly unique atmosphere. Since our number one concern is consumers, we respect every customer and their individual needs.

As a true "neighborhood shop." The Sister's Barber offers both a style and community experience. Our social atmosphere provides our clients with an opportunity to relax and enjoy their surroundings while enjoying a personalized level of service. Our barbers and stylists blend new, cutting-edge grooming methods with conventional timeless services.


Covid-19 Precautious

We have had to change the way we do business, like any organization during the pandemic, to survive and provide our clientele with the best possible service. During these tough times, we sincerely appreciate your patience and understanding. Here is a list of several improvements to keep people safe that have been made.

  • Our shop remain open 7 days a weeks but with limited capacity to maintain social distancing.
  • Online booking appointment to ensure there no clash between the time.
  • Please arrive earlier or no more than 10 minutes late to avoid the clashing with other customers.
  • Wear mask, scan the QR Code and get your temperature check at the entrance.
  • We are operating cashless to avoid handling cash unless unavoidable reason.
